What is FuncTree?

FuncTree helps you visualize and explore omics data onto functional hierarchies like KEGG Brite. Its interactive Tree Viewer highlights trends at multiple functional levels simultaneously to help you formulate scientific hypothesis. Statistical tests, module pathway reconstruction, and different customization controls are built-in to assist you in your quest.

Data Visualization

FuncTree displays functional hierarchies as a radial tree where nodes represent a biological function (e.g. KEGG KO, KEGG Module) and are layered nicely to reflect the different functional levels
